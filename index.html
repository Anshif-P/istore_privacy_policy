Contact Support

Email us at istoredigital.wish@gmail.com for any issues or questions.

Please include: your company name, the feature you need help with, steps to reproduce (if it’s a bug), and relevant screenshots or PDFs.

Hours & Response Times

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. Complex requests may take longer; we’ll keep you updated.

Common Topics

Leads & Sales Goals: Questions on tracking, reports, and analytics.

Tasks & Activity Logs: Help with creating, assigning, and reviewing tasks or activity history.

Access & Permissions: Assistance with sign-in, team access, and storage permissions.

Troubleshooting

Try these quick steps: ensure you have the latest app version, refresh or restart the app, verify network connectivity, and confirm required permissions are granted.

Account & Data Requests

For account updates or data-related requests (access, correction, deletion), contact us via email and we’ll guide you through the process.
